M.A.G.V.I-Multi-Functional Glasses

Visually Impaired people face troubles due to inaccessible infrastructure and social challenges. Let us have an empathetic look at some of the daily life problems, struggles, and challenges faced by blind people. The biggest challenge for a blind person, especially one with a complete loss of vision, is to navigate around places. Blind people roam easily around their house without any help because they know the position of everything in the house. People living with and visiting blind people must make sure not to move things around without informing or asking the blind person. The most valuable thing for a disabled person is gaining independence. A blind person can lead an independent life with some specifically designed adaptive things for them. So I am also designing an adaptive device that can help them.
